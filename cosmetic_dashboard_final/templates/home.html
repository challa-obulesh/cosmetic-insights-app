Cosmetic Insights

“Cosmetic Insights: Navigating Cosmetics Trends and Consumer Insights with Tableau” is an innovative project aimed at revolutionizing how cosmetics data is visualized and utilized to drive informed decision-making and enhance brand success in the beauty industry.

💡 Project Objective

  • Empower stakeholders with actionable information using interactive dashboards.
  • Provide insights into consumer preferences, product effectiveness, and market dynamics.

📊 What We Did

  • Used Tableau to create interactive visualizations.
  • Analyzed product ratings, ingredient trends, and skin type suitability.
  • Prepared dashboards and storyboards for easy insights.
  • Integrated everything into a Flask web application.

📚 Key Scenarios

  1. Monitoring Consumer Preferences: Real-time trends on product popularity and ingredients.
  2. Product Concerns: Quick analysis of negative feedback or safety concerns.
  3. Predictive Analysis: Forecasting trends to innovate future products.

🛠️ Technologies Used

Python, Flask, Tableau Public, Bootstrap

🌐 Dashboards Available

  • Top Brands & Average Ratings
  • Price vs. Rating
  • Ingredient Analysis
  • Skin Type Insights
